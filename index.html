Bigg Boss has been one of the most entertaining reality show on Indian television. The show, which has been a hit for eleven years in its Hindi version, is now venturing into the Marathi industry. Yes! Get ready to witness the same craziness and zeal of Bigg Boss in Marathi language as the makers are all set to bring the Marathi version of the show.

About BIGG BOSS MARATHI

Bigg Boss has gained popularity over the years in all three languages of its telecast. The show hosted by popular actors in Tamil, Telugu and Kannada has been loved.

Keeping the popularity in mind Endemol Shine is working on bringing Bigg Boss Marathi.
